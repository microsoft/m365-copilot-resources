Generally available

  • Researcher — tackles complex, multi-step research tasks and delivers comprehensive, cited reports in hours instead of days | FAQ
  • Analyst — turns raw data into insights and visualizations; works with spreadsheets and data files without requiring any coding knowledge
  • Facilitator — keeps everyone aligned during Teams meetings with shared live notes, captured decisions, and action items
  • Interpreter — lets meeting participants speak and hear in their own language, breaking down language barriers across global teams
  • Employee Self-Service — helps employees instantly answer HR and IT questions, resolve issues, and complete common requests on their own

Public preview

  • Project Manager — automates project planning in Planner, from creating plans and assigning tasks to generating status reports
  • Agents in Channels — Teams channel experts grounded in your team's conversations and meetings, helping everyone stay on top of tasks
  • Agents in Communities — accelerates knowledge sharing in Viva Engage communities by making expertise readily available across your organization

Frontier

  • People — helps you prepare for upcoming meetings, draws on past interactions, and finds colleagues with specific skills
  • Workforce Insights — helps leaders understand how their organization is managed, structured, and staffed to inform workforce decisions
  • Learning — offers personalized Copilot tips based on your current work and surfaces curated learning paths to build new skills
